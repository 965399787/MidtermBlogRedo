Pac-Man - Good v Bad Ai

An Emcompassing World

John Tong | May, 13, 2022

In Pac-Man, each ghost has an assigned color and behavior. Some will follow your exact path or where you're headed or where you could potential be.

The challenge and fun of the game comes from simple mechanics that are complimented by the narrow map design and ghosts' teamwork to respond according to the player and the map.
