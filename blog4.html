Breath of the Wild - NES Demo

The prototype to a Masterpiece

John Tong | May, 13, 2022

The jump to Breath of the Wild was a great risk. To help demonstrate the desired direction for the new Zelda title, the Zelda team created an NES Demo to showcase the new physics and interactions with the landscape.

At the 2017 Game Developers Conference (GDC), the Zelda team presented how an interactive enviroment draws immersion. How fire spreads to trees, grass and wooden weapons. How the density of objects determine if they sink or float when in water. The details of Breath of the Wild's physics is natural and set a the standard for level design.
