Firewatch - Art is Real

An appropriate appreciation for all, ugly and unique, art

John Tong | May, 13, 2022

Firewatch stunned the video game community through its visuals and mystery. A behind-the-scenes look from 2015 GDC shows how the team blended 2D graphics into a 3D environment.

Based around the position of the sun, the color palette shift and intensity of colors would make the cycles of the days in Firewatch feel as if it would continue orbiting without the player there. The gamplay would take a step back for further emphasis on the handwork of Campo Santos and engulf the player in a masterful render ofOregon's wilderness.
